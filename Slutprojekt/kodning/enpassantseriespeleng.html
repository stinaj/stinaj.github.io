Seniors

Seniors (age 20+) play in “allsvenskan” that is played monthly on sundays. Matches are played with 8-man teams with a total of 5 divisions. The time control is 90 minutes + 30 minutes after 40 moves + 30 seconds per move. All matches are elo-rated.

The competition period is currently inactive.

Juniors

Juniors (age up to 20) play in “ungdomsserien” that is played monthly on saturdays. Matches are played with 4-man teams with a total of 4 divisions + the beginners league. The time control in division 1 is 90 minutes + 30 seconds per move. The time control in division 2 is 45 minutes + 15 seconds. The time control in division 3 is 45 minutes. The time control in division 4 is 30 minutes. The time control in the beginners league is … Only division 1 is elo-rated.

The competition period is currently inactive.
