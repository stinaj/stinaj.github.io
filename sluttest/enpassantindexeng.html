En Passant is a chess club in Svedala that was established back in 1976. The club has evolved from a small, insignificant club to a merited club that has climbed all the way from division 6 to “elitserien”.

Regular activities

Monday 18:30 - 22:00
Senior activities with training as well as competitions. Every second Monday EP Elo is played, and the first monday of every month a blitz-tournament with time handicaps is played.

Tuesday 18:00 - 19:15
Training for beginners, going through the basics and fundamentals of chess.
Coach: Johan Hallbeck

Wednesday 18:00 - 19:15
Training for younger juniors, with training topped by competitions.
Coach: Johan Hallbeck, Pontus Fogelberg and Hannes Essfors

Wednesday 19:30 - 21:30
Junior activities on an advanced level with training, analyzes and competitions.
Coach: Roland Johansson and Kent Liljegren

EP Elo

Elo-tournament that is arranged yearly with players from all around Skåne. The time control is 90 minutes + 30 seconds per move.

EP Snabb-Elo

Rapid elo-tournament with players from all around Skåne. The time control is 10 minutes + 5 seconds per move.
