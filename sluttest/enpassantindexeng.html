En Passant is a chess club in Svedala that was established in 1976. The club has gone from a small onconspicuous club into an merited club who has climbed all the way from division 6 in allsvenskan up to elitserien.

Regular activities

Mondays 18.30 - 22.00
Senioractivity with competitions as practices. Every other monday the EP Elo is played and the first monday a month a handicap tournament is played where lower rated players gets time penalty.

Tuesdays 18.00 - 19.15
Beginner activity with practise of the basics of chess and any club competitions.
Coaches: Johan Hallbeck

Wednesdays 18.00 - 19.15
Youth activity for younger ones with practice interspersed with club competitions.
Coaches: Johan Hallbeck, Pontus Fogelberg och Hannes Essfors

Wednesdays 19.30 - 21.00
Youth activity at a higher level with practice, reviews of played games and club competitions.
Coaches: Roland Johansson och Kent Liljegren

EP Elo

Eloranked tournament as the club organizes each year with players all around Scania. Time for concideration is 90 minutes + 30 seconds increment.

EP Rapid-Elo

Rapideloranked tournament as the club organizes with players all around Scania. Time for concideration is 10 minutes + 5 seconds increment.
