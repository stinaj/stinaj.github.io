Seniors

Seniors (age 20+) play in allsvenskan which is played a sunday a month. It is a team competition in Scania with 8-player teams in three divisions and above them Superettan and Elitserien. Time for consideration in all divisions is 90 minutes + 30 minutes after 40 moves + 30 seconds increment. All of allsvenskans divisions are eloranked.

The competition period is currently inactive.

Youth

Youth (age up to 20) play in ungdomsserien which is played a saturday a month. It is a team competition in Scania with 4-player teams in four divisions and below them Nybörjarserien. Time for consideration in division 1 is 90 minutes + 30 seconds increment. Time for consideration in division 2 is 45 minutes + 15 seconds increment. Time for consideration in division 3 is 45 minutes. Time for consideration in division 4 is 30 minutes. Time for consideration in nybörjarserien is ... Only division 1 is eloranked.

The competition period is currently inactive.
